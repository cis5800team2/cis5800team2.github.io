2/18/21 During the team meeting that took place on (2/18), meeting attendees discussed a variety of ideas for the team’s deliverables for the Spring 2021 semester. Ideas discussed included a restaurant pick-up app, a package/shipment tracker, COVID-19 vaccine site tracker, an immigration resource app, a self-checkout app for the Baruch bookstore, and a social animal adoption app. Ultimately, meeting attendees finalized the idea of FoodScape, a social user-based recipe webapp with a unanimous vote. Meeting attendees then divided the presentation design deliverables and set a date for the next meeting, (3/4).
3/4/21 During the team meeting that took place on (3/4), meeting attendees discussed a variety of different website templates and tools such as Wix, figma, Googie Sites, and HTML templates. Ultimately, meeting attendees decided to use an HTML template with the design theme “Theory”, after a unanimous vote. Meeting attendees then explored different project management tool options, and decided upon Trello for its ease of use with a unanimous vote. Meeting attendees then divided tasks that were assigned by the professor, as well as additional tasks to complete an individual about me description and draft a team logo. The next meeting was set for (3/6).
3/6/21 During the team meeting that took place on (3/6), meeting attendees individually updated the rest of the team of their progress and asked any questions that have come up. Meeting attendees discussed how to update and customize the GitHub template effectively. Meeting attendees discussed how to equally split the presentation and a brief explanation of what needs to be presented for each individual part.
